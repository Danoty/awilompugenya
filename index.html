About Awilo

Awilo is a focused, approachable, and visionary leader committed to championing the aspirations of the people of Ugenya with humility, courage, and action.

He believes leadership should create opportunity, restore trust, and improve the daily lives of wananchi through development that is visible, inclusive, and sustainable.

Campaign Message

This campaign is about more than winning a seat. It is about giving Ugenya stronger representation, a clearer development agenda, and leadership that remains answerable to the people.
