About Awilo

Awilo is a dedicated and visionary leader who believes in servant leadership, transparency, and development that touches the lives of ordinary people. His desire to lead Ugenya Constituency is anchored in a strong commitment to improve livelihoods, strengthen public service delivery, and create more opportunities for the youth, women, farmers, and business community.

Vision

To build a united, empowered, and prosperous Ugenya Constituency where every resident has access to quality education, healthcare, economic opportunity, and responsive leadership.

Mission

To provide transformative leadership for Ugenya by championing practical solutions, promoting inclusive development, empowering communities, and ensuring accountability in the use of public resources.

Personality

Awilo is known as a humble, approachable, hardworking, and listening leader. He values people, respects diverse opinions, and believes that leadership should be about service rather than personal gain. He is passionate about justice, fairness, and creating lasting impact in society.

Background

Awilo comes from a strong background of community engagement and public service. He understands the daily realities facing the people of Ugenya, including unemployment, education challenges, healthcare gaps, poor infrastructure, and limited economic opportunities. Through his experience in leadership and community development, he has built a reputation as a practical and people-focused mobilizer for change.
