About Awilo

Awilo is a focused, approachable, and visionary leader committed to championing the aspirations of the people of Ugenya with humility, courage, and practical action.

He believes leadership should expand opportunity, restore trust, and improve the daily lives of wananchi through development that is visible, inclusive, and sustainable.
