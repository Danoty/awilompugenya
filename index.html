About Awilo

A focused, visionary, and approachable leader committed to championing the needs of the people of Ugenya with dignity, courage, and action.

Leadership rooted in the people

Awilo stands for a new generation of responsive leadership that puts people first. This campaign is about restoring trust, expanding opportunities, and ensuring that every ward and every family in Ugenya feels represented, respected, and supported.

With a heart for service and a clear commitment to development, Awilo seeks to transform Ugenya through practical leadership, visible impact, and inclusive decision-making.

Accessible Leadership A leader who listens to wananchi and acts on their priorities.
Development Focus Committed to education, health, roads, water, trade, and youth empowerment.
Transparent Governance Public service with integrity, openness, and accountability.

Vision & Mission

A clear roadmap for transforming Ugenya into a stronger, fairer, and more prosperous constituency.

Vision

To build a united, empowered, and progressive Ugenya where every resident has access to opportunity, quality services, and leadership they can trust.

Mission

To serve the people of Ugenya with integrity and purpose by championing inclusive development, youth and women empowerment, better public services, and accountable representation in Parliament.

Manifesto Priorities

A practical, people-first agenda focused on the everyday needs and long-term progress of Ugenya.

Education Support

Promote fair bursary allocation, support schools, improve learning facilities, and expand opportunities for students.

Youth Empowerment

Create pathways for jobs, innovation, digital skills, sports growth, and entrepreneurship for the youth.

Healthcare Access

Advocate for better-equipped health centres, medicine availability, maternal care, and stronger community health services.

Roads & Infrastructure

Push for improved roads, bridges, public lighting, and connectivity to ease transport and unlock growth.

Water & Sanitation

Support clean water access, sanitation projects, and healthier living conditions across the constituency.

Agriculture & Trade

Empower farmers, support small traders, strengthen market access, and promote value addition for local produce.

Women Empowerment

Expand economic opportunities, support groups and enterprises, and strengthen women’s voice in leadership.

Transparent Leadership

Build trust through open communication, accountable representation, and regular engagement with the people.
